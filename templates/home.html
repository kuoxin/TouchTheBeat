TouchTheBeat

TouchTheBeat is a HTML5 multi-touch music game currently in development.

Try TouchTheBeat »

The Game

TouchTheBeat is level-based. Each level consists of a song and a composition of interactive objects. The objects can be either tapped, hold, dragged or slided (soon). The more rhythmically accurate you interact with the objects, the more points you get in the end.

The Level-Builder

You can easily create your own levels by using any SoundCloud track as music for your level. When finished, you might share and exchange your levels online in text-format. This way it is easy to play levels created by others.
